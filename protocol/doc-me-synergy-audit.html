1. The Electro-Erosion Threat

WARNING: Electrical failures (poor grounding) directly transfer energy into the mechanical system. This "Synergy Gap" causes rapid bearing failure.

The goal is zero stray current on the shaft line. Any measurable voltage above 0.5V is considered a critical non-conformance.

2. Diagnostic Protocol

Phase A: Grounding

  • Grid Resistance: ≤ 1 Ohm
  • Bonding Integrity Check
  • Shaft Current Measurement

Phase B: Correlation

  • Thermography (Hot Spots)
  • Vibration Spectral Analysis
  • Harmonic Distortion Check

3. Mitigation Strategy

Corrective actions include mandatory installation of electrically insulated bearings and permanent shaft grounding brushes.
