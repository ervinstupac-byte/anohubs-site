LCC & Procurement Veto

REF: DOC-FIN-003 | REV: 1.1

1. The Cost of "Cheap"

This protocol mandates a comprehensive **Life Cycle Cost (LCC)** analysis. We actively veto procurement decisions based solely on lowest CAPEX if the material integrity audit predicts failure within the first 30% of the asset's lifecycle.

2. Material Compatibility

Preventing Galvanic Corrosion is our primary material directive. We verify all alloys for potential electrochemical reactions.

VETO TRIGGER

Any mix of metals with a potential difference > 0.25V in the operating environment triggers an immediate procurement halt.
