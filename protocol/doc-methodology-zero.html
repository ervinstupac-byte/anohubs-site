1. Forensic NDT Protocols

We employ a multi-spectrum approach to identify defects before they become failures.

Ultrasonic Phased Array (PAUT)

Advanced imaging of internal structure. Essential for checking weld integrity on Francis runners and Kaplan blades.

Magnetic Particle (MPI)

Detection of surface and near-surface cracks in ferromagnetic materials. Mandatory for shaft inspections.

2. Bio-Inspired Immunity

We validate the **Dual Application Strategy** (Tubercle Technology) through advanced CFD. This reduces drag and flow separation by generating counter-rotating vortices.

CFD Validation Checklist:

  • Grid Independence Study completed.
  • Turbulence Model: k-omega SST.
  • Transient simulation (not steady-state).

3. Ethical Sourcing Mandate

Technical protocols are upheld by ethical discipline. We mandate strict material traceability. Every component must have a "Digital Passport" verifying its origin, material composition, and manufacturing date.
