Precision Alignment Protocol

REF: DOC-ALIGN-005 | REV: 2.0

1. Executive Summary

This document defines the mandatory specifications for turbine-generator shaft alignment. The objective is to eliminate the 48% dynamic instability risk caused by the "Organizational Execution Gap."

CRITICAL MANDATE:

Alignment must achieve a tolerance of 0.05 mm per meter. Standard "fast alignment" techniques are strictly prohibited.

2. Methodology & Tolerances

Measurements must be performed using calibrated laser systems. Dial indicators are permitted only for secondary verification.

Parameter AnoHUB Tolerance Risk of Deviation
Shaft Radial Runout ≤ 0.01 mm Bearing overheating.
Shaft Concentricity ≤ 0.05 mm/m Dynamic Instability (48% Risk).
Generator Air Gap ± 5% Nominal Magnetic pull & vibration.

3. Post-Assembly Validation

Validation is not complete until dynamic load testing confirms thermal stability and vibration signatures below ISO 10816 Zone A limits.
