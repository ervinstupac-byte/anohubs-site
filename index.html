[Placeholder: Francis Turbine Image]

Francis Turbine

Ideal for medium head and flow rates, the Francis turbine is the most common. Our audits ensure **optimal blade geometry** and flow dynamics, maximizing efficiency and lifespan.

  • Focus: CFD Optimization & Acoustic Emission (Cavitation)
[Placeholder: Kaplan Turbine Image]

Kaplan Turbine

A propeller-type turbine for low head and high flow rates. Our analysis focuses on **adjustable blade performance** and **cavitation prevention**, crucial for maintaining peak output.

  • Focus: Adjustable Blade Performance & Real-time Load Simulation
[Placeholder: Pelton Turbine Image]

Pelton Turbine

Designed for very high head and low flow applications. We provide integrity checks on the **nozzle and bucket systems**, ensuring impulse energy conversion is precise and wear is minimized.

  • Focus: Nozzle System Precision & Impulse Energy Conversion
