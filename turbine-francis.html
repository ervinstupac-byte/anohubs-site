A Legacy of Efficiency and Power

The Francis turbine, named after James B. Francis who perfected the design in 1848, stands as a true powerhouse of engineering simplicity and efficiency. It is the workhorse of the hydropower world, perfectly designed to handle **medium water heads** and a wide range of flow rates. This versatility allows it to be used globally, adapting to diverse hydrological conditions.
