The Francis Challenge: Pressure and Flow Dynamics

The Francis turbine operates under immense hydraulic pressure where efficiency hinges on maintaining perfect flow over the complex runner geometry. Our specialized audits ensure that this geometry is optimal and that pressure differentials do not lead to localized structural failure.

Focus Protocol: Maximizing Lifespan

  • CFD Optimization:

    Utilizing Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) to validate and adjust blade profiles.

  • Acoustic Emission Monitoring (AEM):

    Non-invasive acoustic monitoring to detect the precise onset of cavitation.

  • Partial Load Instability:

    Auditing turbine performance during off-design operation.
