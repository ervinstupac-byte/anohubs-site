Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Anohubs ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our website and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

1.1. Information You Provide:

When you contact us via our contact forms (powered by Formspree), we collect personal information you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Name and surname.
  • Business email address.
  • Company name.
  • Message content.

We use this information solely to respond to your inquiries and establish business communication.

1.2. Automatically Collected Information (Cookies & Analytics):

Our website uses minimal analytics and cookies to monitor performance.

  • **Analytics:** We collect anonymous data regarding page visits, time spent on the site, and general geographic location. This data helps us optimize user experience and cannot be used to personally identify you.
  • **Cookies:** We use technical cookies necessary for the basic functionality of the website. By using our site, you consent to the use of these essential cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your requests and provide the services you seek (primary purpose).
  • To improve our website and service offerings.
  • To analyze usage trends and site activity.
  • To prevent fraudulent or illegal activities.

3. Sharing of Information

Anohubs **does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information** to third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share your information only with trusted service providers who perform functions on our behalf (e.g., Formspree for email delivery), who are bound by confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our contact form or directly at:

Email: privacy@anohubs.com
