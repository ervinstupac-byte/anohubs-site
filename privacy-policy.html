Privacy Policy

How we protect and manage your data within the AnoHUB ecosystem.

LAST UPDATED: NOVEMBER 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Anohubs ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our website and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your operational data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

1.1. Information You Provide

When you contact us via our contact forms, we collect personal information you voluntarily provide (Name, Business Email, Company Name, Message Content) solely to respond to your inquiries.

1.2. Automated Information (Cookies)

We collect anonymous analytics data (page visits, time on site) to optimize user experience. We use essential technical cookies necessary for the basic functionality of the website.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your requests and provide the services you seek (primary purpose).
  • To improve our website and service offerings based on user behavior.
  • To analyze usage trends and prevent fraudulent activity.

3. Information Sharing

Anohubs does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data only with trusted service providers (e.g., email delivery services) who are strictly bound by confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement robust administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us directly:

privacy@anohubs.com
