The Pelton Challenge

The Pelton Turbine operates on microscopic precision. Maximum efficiency relies entirely on the precise geometry of the water jet and the impact angle on the runner buckets. Any deviation in nozzle alignment leads to immediate efficiency loss.

Focus Protocol

  • Nozzle System Precision: Micro-level alignment checks of the jet needle and nozzle opening.
  • Bucket Wear Analysis: Non-destructive testing (NDT) and 3D scanning to quantify abrasion.
  • Abrasion Mitigation: Material recommendations (Ceramic/Carbide) for high-sediment environments.
