The Pelton Challenge: Impulse and Wear

Designed for high-head applications, the Pelton turbine's efficiency depends entirely on the precise conversion of water's kinetic energy into impulse force. This reliance makes the nozzle and bucket systems acutely vulnerable to high-velocity particle impact (abrasion) and any loss of precision in the jet alignment.
