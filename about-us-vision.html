OUR VISION FOR HYDRO INTEGRITY

We are pioneers, translating complex inspection data into clear, actionable strategies that ensure the longevity, efficiency, and safety of your hydraulic assets.

Our Core Mission Pillars

The foundation of every analysis we perform is built on three uncompromisable values.

Microscopic Precision

We utilize 3D scanning and sub-surface NDT techniques to achieve measurement accuracy that exceeds industry standards, eliminating guesswork in damage assessment.

Predictive Reliability

Our data is processed through custom AI models, moving beyond simple diagnostics to predict future failure points, optimizing maintenance schedules and reducing downtime.

Sustainable Longevity

By extending the functional lifespan of existing infrastructure, we significantly reduce capital expenditure and promote environmentally responsible hydro energy production.

A History of Innovation

Anohubs was founded by a team of hydraulic engineers and data scientists frustrated by the limitations of conventional turbine inspection methods. We built a platform that closes the gap between complex physical data and simplified, critical business decisions.

Our proprietary methodologies, utilizing sensors and analytics developed in-house, provide a complete digital twin of your assets. This allows for unparalleled monitoring of cavitation, erosion, and fatigue—the silent killers of hydro efficiency.
