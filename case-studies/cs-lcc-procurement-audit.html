Procurement Integrity: LCC Audit Secures 30-Year Value

**Client Profile:** Large Public Utility | **Problem:** Low-Bid Trap | **Solution:** Material Veto | **Outcome:** 18% LCC Savings.

1. The Challenge: The Cost of Cheap

The client was about to sign a contract with the lowest bidder for a critical component overhaul. The winning bid was 20% cheaper than the competition. However, our pre-contract audit revealed that this low CapEx price was achieved by specifying suboptimal materials and coatings.

Specifically, the supplier proposed mixing incompatible metals in a high-moisture environment, creating a high risk of **Galvanic Corrosion** that would necessitate replacement within 5-7 years.

2. The AnoHUB Protocol: LCC Audit & Integrity Veto

  • LCC Modeling: Developed a verifiable financial model demonstrating that the "low bid" would actually result in **18% higher costs** over 30 years due to mandatory maintenance and downtime.
  • Material Compatibility Audit: Identified the precise chemical risk of galvanic corrosion linked to the proposed material mixing, validating the Veto decision.
  • Procurement Veto: Provided the executive team with irrefutable data to reject the low-bid contract in favor of a provider guaranteeing 30-year material integrity.
