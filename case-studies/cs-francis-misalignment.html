98% Availability: Neutralizing Hidden Dynamic Risk

**Problem:** Recurring Vibration | **Solution:** Precision Alignment (0.05mm) | **Outcome:** 48% Risk Eliminated.

1. The Challenge: Invisible Instability

The plant (Francis, 25MW) experienced frequent unplanned shutdowns and accelerating bearing wear (guide bearings lasting only 18 months). Previous service providers attributed the issue to "normal component fatigue" and recommended standard replacements.

Our initial audit revealed a systemic Organizational Execution Gap: The shaft alignment deviation exceeded 0.15 mm/m, creating a latent dynamic instability (the 48% risk factor) that no bearing replacement could fix.

2. The AnoHUB Protocol: Root Cause Correction

  • Forensic NDT Audit: Acoustic monitoring pinpointed specific vibration harmonics (2x RPM) indicative of misalignment, ruling out hydraulic imbalance.
  • Precision Alignment: We enforced the non-negotiable 0.05 mm/m tolerance on the shaft assembly using dual-laser systems.
  • M-E Synergy Check: Identified hidden grounding issues in the generator, eliminating stray currents that were accelerating bearing wear via electro-erosion.
