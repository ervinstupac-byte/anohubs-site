Abrasion Risk Zeroed: Pelton Nozzle & Bucket Integrity

**Client Profile:** High-head Pelton Turbine, South American region | **Challenge Duration:** Annual downtime due to severe bucket pitting | **AnoHUB Intervention:** Q1 2025.

1. The Challenge: High-Velocity Abrasion and Premature Failure

The plant operates in a high-sediment environment, causing extreme high-velocity abrasion that required the Pelton buckets and nozzles to be refurbished annually. This reactive cycle led to excessive OpEx and significant revenue loss from constant downtime.

  • **Key Symptom:** Thinning of bucket splitters and loss of optimal nozzle geometry.
  • **Hidden Risk:** Inadequate sediment bypass system and use of standard stainless steel alloys.

2. The AnoHUB Protocol: Material Science and Precision NDT

The solution involved a dual approach: advanced material science and hydraulic component precision:

  1. **Forensic NDT Analysis:** Used sub-surface testing (NDT) to identify micro-cracks before they became visible.
  2. **Nozzle Precision Check:** Verified the spear and nozzle alignment to ensure the water jet maintained perfect laminar flow.
  3. **Material Upgrade:** Recommended and oversaw the application of specialized ceramic-metal composite coatings.
