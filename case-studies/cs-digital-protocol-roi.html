SCADA-to-CEO Gap Closed: Verifiable ROI on Digital Protocol

**Client Profile:** Utility (15 Units) | **Problem:** Unquantifiable Risk | **Solution:** Hydro Health Index | **Outcome:** 15% Insurance Savings.

1. The Challenge: Data Rich, Information Poor

The utility struggled to translate raw SCADA and sensor data into actionable financial metrics for executive decision-makers. They generated terrabytes of data but lacked a standardized framework to prove low operational risk to insurers and shareholders.

This disconnect (the **SCADA-to-CEO Gap**) led to excessively high insurance premiums and inefficient, calendar-based maintenance scheduling that wasted labor hours.

2. The AnoHUB Protocol: Quantifying Integrity

  • Hydro Health Index (HHI): We aggregated complex technical data into a single, verifiable risk score (0-100) for each asset, giving executives instant visibility into portfolio health.
  • Insurance Risk Quantification: Provided insurers with access to verifiable, real-time integrity reports, proving a lower risk profile backed by immutable data logs.
  • Risk-Based Maintenance: Shifted from time-based to condition-based maintenance, reducing unnecessary interventions on healthy machines.
