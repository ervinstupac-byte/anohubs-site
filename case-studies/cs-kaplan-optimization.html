+3.1% Efficiency Gain Post-Modernization

**Client Profile:** Low-head Kaplan Fleet | **Problem:** Efficiency Loss | **Solution:** Hub Optimization | **Outcome:** +3.1% Gain.

1. The Challenge: The Performance Gap

The client undertook a modernization of their 40-year-old Kaplan fleet, but conventional alignment and refurbishment methods failed to restore original hydraulic efficiency targets. Efficiency plateaued at 88%, well below the 91% design goal.

We identified the root cause: **Hydraulic Hub Wear**. Invisible during static checks, hydraulic play in the blade pitch mechanism caused dynamic flow separation at partial loads.

2. The AnoHUB Protocol: Precision & Digital Twin

  • Digital Twin CFD: We ran high-fidelity CFD simulations to identify the optimal blade angles and guide vane opening settings specifically for the site's current hydrological conditions.
  • Hub Precision Check: Used non-destructive testing (NDT) to measure wear in control rods, leading to a micrometre-precision re-machining plan to eliminate play.
  • LCC Procurement Audit: Advised on upgrading to self-lubricating bushings to minimize future maintenance cycles and eliminate oil pollution risk.
