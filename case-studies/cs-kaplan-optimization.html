+3.1% Efficiency Gain Post-Modernization

**Client Profile:** Low-head Kaplan Turbine Fleet, 40-year old units | **Challenge Duration:** 12 months of high LCC | **AnoHUB Intervention:** Q4 2024.

1. The Challenge: Hydraulic Inefficiency and Blade Pitch Errors

The client was undertaking a modernization, but conventional alignment and refurbishment methods failed to restore original hydraulic efficiency targets. We identified two primary causes for the persistent performance gap: hydraulic hub wear causing blade pitch errors and minor component fatigue.

  • **Key Symptom:** Efficiency plateaued at 88%, well below the 91% design goal.
  • **Hidden Risk:** Hydraulic play in the blade pitch mechanism, invisible during static checks, caused dynamic flow separation at partial load.

2. The AnoHUB Protocol: CFD Calibration and LCC Audit

We applied an optimization strategy focused on the hydraulic integrity of the rotating assembly:

  1. **Digital Twin CFD Calibration:** Performed a high-fidelity CFD simulation to identify optimal blade angles and guide vane opening settings.
  2. **Hydraulic Hub Precision Check:** Used non-destructive testing (NDT) to measure wear and play in the control rods, achieving micrometre precision alignment.
  3. **LCC Procurement Audit:** Advised on long-term material choices to minimize future maintenance cycles.
