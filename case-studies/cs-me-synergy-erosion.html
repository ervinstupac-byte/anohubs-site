Electro-Erosion Stopped: M-E Synergy Audit Success

**Client Profile:** Vertical Pelton Unit, Generator bearing failure | **Challenge Duration:** Recurring bearing replacements due to unknown causes | **AnoHUB Intervention:** Q1 2025.

1. The Challenge: Bearing Failures Caused by Stray Currents

The client faced consistent micro-pitting and catastrophic failures in their generator bearings, despite using high-quality components and optimal lubrication. The root cause was entirely electrical, a flaw in the M-E Synergy.

  • **Key Symptom:** 'Washing' appearance and tiny pits on the bearing race surface (classic electro-erosion sign).
  • **Hidden Risk:** Improperly bonded grounding cables forced stray electric currents through the least resistant path—the shaft and bearings.

2. The AnoHUB Protocol: M-E Synergy Audit and Grounding Correction

We deployed the specialized M-E Synergy Protocol to diagnose the hidden electrical fault:

  1. **Forensic Electrical Audit:** Used specialized equipment to trace stray currents across the generator assembly.
  2. **Grounding System Re-Bonding:** Corrected inadequate bonding on critical generator components, redirecting currents away from the shaft.
  3. **Bearing Replacement with Insulation:** Installed new, electrically insulated bearings as a permanent measure against future current leaks.
