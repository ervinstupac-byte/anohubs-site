Electro-Erosion Stopped: M-E Synergy Audit Success

**Problem:** Recurring Bearing Failure | **Solution:** M-E Synergy Audit | **Outcome:** 5+ Year Life Extension.

1. The Challenge: The Synergy Gap

The client (Vertical Pelton Unit) faced consistent micro-pitting and catastrophic failures in their generator bearings, despite using high-quality components and optimal lubrication.

The root cause was entirely electrical: improper grounding cables created a "Synergy Gap," forcing stray electric currents to find the path of least resistance through the shaft and bearings, causing **Electro-Discharge Machining (EDM)** damage.

2. The Solution: Comprehensive M-E Protocol

  • Forensic Electrical Audit: Used specialized brushes and oscilloscopes to trace stray currents (shaft voltages) across the generator assembly.
  • Grounding Re-Bonding: Corrected inadequate bonding on critical generator components to redirect currents to ground, neutralizing the root cause.
  • Insulated Bearings: Installed new, electrically insulated bearings as a permanent physical barrier against future current leaks.
