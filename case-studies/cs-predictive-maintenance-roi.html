Predictive Certainty: 100% Elimination of Unplanned Downtime

**Problem:** Recurring Surprise Failures | **Solution:** Digital Twin AI | **Outcome:** Zero Downtime (3 Years).

1. The Challenge: Unpredictable Failures

The client (Francis/Kaplan Fleet) struggled with recurring, unpredictable outages caused by issues like minor bearing wear and transient electrical faults. Traditional preventative maintenance failed because these **dynamic failures** escalated too rapidly between scheduled checks.

The core problem was the **low alarm-to-failure rate**: alarms triggered only when failure was imminent, offering zero lead time for planned intervention.

2. The AnoHUB Protocol: Digital Twin & AI Logic

  • Digital Twin Synchronization: Installed high-fidelity acoustic and vibration sensors to feed continuous, granular data into the virtual replica for real-time comparison.
  • AI Anomaly Detection: Implemented proprietary ML models trained on normal operational data, allowing the system to predict minute deviations (the true **Anomaly Detection** markers) before they reach critical limits.
  • Condition-Based Maintenance: Maintenance shifted to intervention only when AI confidence exceeded 70%, guaranteeing lead time of **15 to 30 days**.
