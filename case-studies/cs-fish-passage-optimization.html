Symbiosis Success: Ecological Flow Verification

**Problem:** License Risk & Public Pressure | **Solution:** Digital Verification | **Outcome:** 100% Compliance & Trust.

1. The Challenge: Proving Integrity

A Run-of-River plant faced difficulties securing a permanent operating license due to public opposition and regulatory scrutiny. Regulators cited failures in demonstrating continuous adherence to **Ecological Flow (E-Flow)** standards, relying on outdated, manual sampling that lacked credibility.

The hidden risk was not just legal, but reputational: a perception that the plant was prioritizing profit over river health, threatening its social license to operate.

2. The AnoHUB Protocol: The Symbiosis Standard

  • Digital Flow Verification: Installed high-precision, tamper-proof flow sensors in bypass sections and minimum flow outlets, providing continuous, time-stamped proof of compliance.
  • Fish Passage Optimization: Conducted transient flow analysis within the existing fish ladder to ensure optimal velocity and minimized turbulence, improving passage efficiency for local species.
  • Automated Reporting: Enabled the AnoHUB platform to generate instant compliance reports, linking operational data directly to regulatory requirements.
