Core Inspection Pillars

Our integrity check is driven by quantifiable precision across all machine components:

  • 0.05 mm/m Mandate:

    Rigorously enforcing the **0.05 mm per meter** precision standard during installation and re-assembly checks to nullify future vibration and costly bearing wear.

  • Digital Twin Validation (CFD/FEA):

    Creating a precise digital replica of your turbine to simulate extreme load scenarios, accurately calibrate safety limits, and predict hydraulic integrity.

  • Forensic NDT & Material Checks:

    Utilizing Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) and mandatory material compatibility audits (crucial against galvanic corrosion) to assess hidden micro-defects.

  • Root Cause Analysis (RCFA):

    Our focus is on identifying the initial problem (e.g., faulty procurement) that caused symptomatic failures (e.g., severe cavitation), ensuring a permanent solution.
